Emergency Preparedness Checklists

Quick reference checklists to help schools and colleges prepare for disasters.

Fire Safety Checklist

  • Identify nearest fire exits
  • Practice fire drills monthly
  • Keep fire extinguishers accessible
  • Check smoke alarms regularly

Earthquake Checklist

  • Practice “Drop, Cover, Hold” drills
  • Secure heavy furniture
  • Keep emergency kits ready
  • Mark safe evacuation routes

Flood Safety Checklist

  • Identify higher ground locations
  • Store clean drinking water
  • Prepare waterproof emergency kits
  • Avoid low-lying areas

Pandemic Checklist

  • Ensure hand sanitizers in classrooms
  • Enforce mask-wearing when needed
  • Maintain social distancing rules
  • Isolate sick students immediately
